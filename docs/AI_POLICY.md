# AI Usage Policy

The Aurora LTFS project welcomes the use of AI tools as part of the development workflow.

## Requirements

- **Disclose AI usage.** When submitting a pull request, state the AI tool you used (e.g., Claude Code, Copilot, Cursor) and the scope of its assistance.

- **Understand your code.** You must fully comprehend all code you submit. If asked, you should be able to explain what your changes do and how they interact with the rest of the system without AI assistance.

- **Review AI-generated content.** All AI-generated text, code, and documentation must be reviewed and edited by a human before submission. Do not submit raw AI output without review.

## AI is Welcome Here

Aurora LTFS is developed with AI assistance, and Core Members actively use AI tools in their workflow. We believe AI is a valuable productivity tool when used responsibly.

This policy exists not to restrict AI usage, but to ensure that all contributions are well-understood by their authors and meet the project's quality standards.
